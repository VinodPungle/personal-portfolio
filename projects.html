INK Content Catalyst — Agentic RAG Platform

Deloitte US · Aug 2025 – Present

Technical Manager — solution architecture, delivery leadership

Deloitte's audit practice maintains complex audit manuals across many formats, and keeping them current is slow, manual, and risk-laden. I lead the discovery-phase team that designed a search-and-update platform on Azure to automate ingestion, extraction, retrieval, and maintenance of that content using a Retrieval-Augmented Generation architecture.

  • Designed an agentic framework: specialised AI agents for document processing, comparison, summarisation, and updates — orchestrated with Azure Functions and Durable Functions.
  • Hybrid retrieval combining semantic and keyword search (Azure Cognitive Search) with Neo4j knowledge graph for multi-hop relationship and dependency mapping — the pattern that keeps generation grounded in high-stakes audit content.
  • Azure Document Intelligence for extraction from PDFs and unstructured formats; Azure OpenAI for contextual summarisation and content generation; Blob Storage, Cosmos DB, and API Management for secure, scalable data handling.
  • Delivered the product backlog, estimates, high-level plan, and budget for the implementation phase.
