IIM Visakhapatnam · April 2025

Alumnus — Indian Institute of Management Visakhapatnam

The Indian Institutes of Management are India's top-ranked institutions for management education, and IIM Visakhapatnam has held the leading position among all new-generation IIMs in the Government of India's NIRF rankings. This five-month executive programme was built for leaders who must take AI from strategy to production:

  • ML foundations — regression, classifiers, decision trees & random forests, SVMs, clustering, neural networks & deep learning, recommender systems.
  • NLP and Large Language Models — sentiment analysis, topic modelling, chatbots and virtual agents, Generative AI and prompt engineering; computer vision fundamentals and applications.
  • Production-Level AI — AI project management and MLOps, cloud ML pipelines, model deployment and production monitoring.
  • AI strategy, regulations, explainability, ethics and privacy — the governance lens for enterprise AI adoption.
  • Case-based pedagogy (Harvard Business School and IIM Bangalore cases) and a hands-on capstone project.
