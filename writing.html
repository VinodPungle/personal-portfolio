Stop Hallucinations in RAG: Why Knowledge Graphs Change Everything

vinood.hashnode.dev

GraphRAG · LLM reliability · Enterprise AI

Pure vector retrieval breaks down in high-stakes domains: semantically similar isn't the same as factually connected, and that gap is where hallucinations live. This piece makes the case for knowledge-graph-grounded retrieval — multi-hop relationships, explicit dependencies, verifiable answers — drawn from building exactly this architecture for enterprise audit content and in my open-source hybrid RAG system.
